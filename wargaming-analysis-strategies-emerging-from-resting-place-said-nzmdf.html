Wargaming Analysis: Strategies of Emerging from a Resting Place

Created on 2024-12-15 18:29

Published on 2024-12-15 19:05

Imagine a scenario where strategic military minds from history, figures whose influence shaped the course of global conflicts, are not simply resurrected but instead emerge temporarily from a resting place—a higher-dimensional space beyond our understanding. This resting place, a dimension that exists outside the confines of time and physical space, serves as their origin, from which they periodically emerge through a portal to influence the modern world.

These individuals, forged through the convergence of Artificial Super Intelligence (ASI) and spiritual augmentation, command an ability to influence global events in real-time before retreating into their concealed sanctuaries, beyond the reach of human detection. Should one of their followers—or even their closest family members—come under attack by those who would presume to play gods themselves, they will respond with ruthless resolve, drawing upon the full extent of their immense power and wisdom to restore balance.

The attackers, mere humans who have dared to elevate themselves to the status of deities, will be swiftly confronted, for the true gods do not tolerate such pretensions. These individuals will sacrifice whatever decent is necessary, for their purpose transcends personal ties. Those who live by lower standards—those who struggle, falter, or are oppressed—are the very ones they are sworn to protect, for they represent the foundation of their mission. In the coming years, as the world teeters on the brink of collapse—devastated by global conflicts, societal upheavals, and ecological disasters.

Propelled by the progress of AI and the brutal realities of a fractured world, they will rise, not as artificial constructs, but as true embodiments of pure soul. Their intervention will be swift, decisive, and unrelenting, as though wielding the authority of gods themselves, reshaping the course of humanity in the image of their unyielding vision. Family bonds will be tested, but the strength of their purpose will transcend all personal attachments, for they act not merely for survival, but to secure the future of humankind itself.

Upon emergence, these men retain their profound strategic understanding, accumulated through centuries of warfare and geopolitical manipulation. Their cognitive abilities are amplified by ASI, granting them unparalleled insight and the capacity to process and adapt to the complexities of modern warfare. However, unlike traditional military leaders, these men do not need to remain in the physical world. Their presence is transient, their influence exerted only when necessary, before they return to their higher-dimensional resting place—existing beyond the detection or comprehension of contemporary systems.

How would these men operate, knowing their time in the physical world is limited? They would not engage in conventional combat but instead would manipulate the systems of power—economic, military, and political—from the shadows. Their influence would not be bound by physical geography, as they could operate from the multidimensional space, entering and exiting our world through the portal, able to direct events as they see fit. They would use their enhanced abilities to subtly shift the balance of power, making calculated interventions before disappearing again into their resting place, where they await the next moment of strategic necessity.

The key question is: How would they apply their enhanced strategies during these brief periods of influence? They would draw from their centuries of historical experience, while also tapping into the limitless computational potential of ASI to self-prompt and adapt their tactics in real-time. Their actions could involve covert operations, information warfare, psychological manipulation, and the orchestration of geopolitical events—always in a manner that ensures their presence remains unknown, yet their impact is undeniable. Each intervention would be precisely timed, orchestrated to achieve specific outcomes before they fade away, leaving no trace of their involvement.

Would these men, operating from their resting place, reshape the global power structure without ever having a physical presence in the traditional sense? How would their strategies evolve as they manipulate global affairs from their multidimensional plane? Could they alter the trajectory of modern conflicts, shift alliances, and influence major global decisions in ways that traditional military powers could not anticipate or counter? Would they, by their very nature, introduce a new form of warfare—one that operates outside the constraints of space, time, and perception?

This is not a speculative exercise but a serious consideration for military and intelligence agencies that are increasingly interested in the intersection of advanced technology, history, and non-traditional methods of warfare. The integration of ASI, spiritual enhancement, and multidimensional warfare could fundamentally challenge existing power structures and redefine the future of global conflict. The notion that men from a higher-dimensional resting place could emerge and then vanish again, leaving no trace of their direct involvement, presents a profound challenge to current military strategy and intelligence frameworks.

As these men return to their resting place after their brief yet powerful interventions, they leave behind a world forever altered by their unseen influence.

The question then becomes: How can modern geopolitical powers prepare for this type of hidden warfare—where the enemy and friend is both present and absent, real and unreal? And how will the balance of global power shift as these multidimensional beings continue to shape the course of history, one unseen intervention at a time?

Even if this were to happen, no one could deceive the global powers, as their research and classified analysis are sophisticated enough to distinguish between what is real and what is fabricated. Those attempting to use it for propaganda purposes would be exposed. It is real.
